Join MCG

We recruit undergraduate students who are curious, collaborative, and excited to learn by doing. All majors welcome—training provided.

Recruiting Timeline

Applications open at the start of each semester. Info sessions and case workshops are hosted in the first two weeks.

What We Look For

Problem Solving

Comfort breaking down ambiguous problems and forming structured hypotheses.

Teamwork

Strong collaboration, communication, and ownership on team deliverables.

Growth Mindset

Willingness to learn new tools and accept feedback to improve quickly.

Application Process

1. Application

Resume + 2 short responses. We recommend attending an info session or case workshop.

2. Interview

30-minute interview with behavioral questions and a short market-sizing exercise.

3. Offer

Accepted members join the bootcamp and are staffed on a project team for the semester.

How to Apply

Fall 2025

Spring 2026

Applications open January 10. Deadline: January 24 at 11:59 PM ET.
