Pro Bono Projects

MCG teams work with nonprofits, social ventures, student founders, and local businesses. Every engagement is an opportunity for students to learn by delivering real value.

How Projects Work

Team Structure

1 Project Lead, 3–5 Consultants, and an Alumni Mentor. Weekly client syncs, internal standups, and deliverable reviews.

Timeline

10–12 weeks per semester with mid-point and final readouts. Clear workplan and scope aligned to client goals.

Deliverables

Executive-ready slides, simple analytics dashboards, and implementation playbooks tailored to each client.
