About MCG Consulting

Founded on the principle that every organization has untapped potential, MCG Consulting has been transforming businesses across industries for over a decade. Our approach combines rigorous analytical thinking with practical implementation expertise to deliver sustainable results.

Our Mission

To empower organizations with strategic insights and operational excellence that create lasting competitive advantages and drive exceptional stakeholder value.

Our Story

MCG was established in 2013 by a group of former McKinsey, BCG, and Bain consultants who shared a vision of creating a more collaborative and results-focused consulting experience. What started as a boutique strategy firm has grown into a global consulting powerhouse, while maintaining our founding commitment to personalized service and measurable outcomes.
