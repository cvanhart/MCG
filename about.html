About MCG

MCG (Michigan Consulting Group) is a student-led, pro bono consulting organization. Our mission is two-fold: deliver meaningful value to community partners and help undergraduates build real consulting, technical, and professional skills through hands-on projects.

What Makes Us Different

We combine structured training with real client work. New members complete a skills bootcamp covering research, problem structuring, slide design, analytics, and communication. Then, students apply this toolkit on client engagements with mentorship from experienced leads.

Pillars of MCG

Student Growth

Workshops, case practice, resume reviews, interview prep, and peer mentorship designed to accelerate your professional development.

Community Impact

All projects are pro bono. We support nonprofits, student ventures, and local businesses with practical strategies and execution.

Team Culture

Inclusive, supportive, and collaborative. We learn by doing, give thoughtful feedback, and celebrate team wins together.

Training & Support

Bootcamp

4–6 sessions covering core consulting skills: problem trees, hypotheses, market sizing, Excel/Sheets, data storytelling, and executive-ready slides.

Analytics

Intro to SQL and Python for analytics. Lightweight tooling with Sheets and Apps Script for project-ready deliverables.

Mentorship

Each project team has a Project Lead and an Alumni Mentor for guidance, career advice, and quality checks.
